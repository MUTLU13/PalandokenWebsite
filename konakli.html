Konakli Skicenter Map



The Konaklı Ski Center, also known as Palandöken2, boasts an additional 24.8 km of slopes in addition to the total number of slopes. The total length of slopes in these two ski resorts reaches 87 km. The longest ski trail is 12.5 km long, allowing uninterrupted skiing throughout. Furthermore, there is a 1,100-meter difference in altitude between the starting and ending elevations.
The Konaklı Ski Center is renowned not only for its magnificent ski slopes but also for numerous exciting activities on the snow. Here are some unforgettable experiences you can have in this white paradise:
Paragliding: Soar through the skies while enjoying the spectacular views of Palandöken.
Giant Swing: Experience the thrill of swinging on a giant swing and take off with a magnificent view over the snow.
Zipline: A high-speed zipline tour offers breathtaking excitement as you glide over the mountains.
Human Slingshot: If you're up for flying over the snow, the human slingshot is just for you.
